Welcome to TeleHero!



TeleHero is an app that can help you filter through the many TeleHealth providers in Ontario to find the one that best fits your needs.

Whether you need to find an online dermatologist to speak with, a doctor to get a referral or a sick note from, or a nurse to speak with anonymously about health matters, TeleHero can help you find the right resource.
